Purpose
-------
CCAR/DFAST regulatory stress testing for market risk loss forecasting.
Used to estimate potential trading book losses under Fed-prescribed
macroeconomic scenarios (severely adverse, adverse, baseline).

Methodology
-----------
Monte Carlo simulation with 10,000 paths.
Applies historically-calibrated stress factors to current portfolio positions.
Key risk factors: interest rates, credit spreads, equity indices, FX rates.
Number of risk factors: 47

----
Primary data source: Global Risk Data Warehouse (GRDW)
Observation period:  Q1 2005 - Q4 2024 (20 years)
Data frequency:      Daily
Key variables:       P&L attribution, VaR breaks, position-level Greeks
